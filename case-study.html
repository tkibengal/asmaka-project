Business Use Cases

Your business use cases can differ – from adding a Data Integration module to a mature platform, to adding a Self-Service Report Builder to an ERP. Regardless of whether you need to utilize a proven Tech Stack to speed up solution delivery in Supply Chain or deploy a 'Services-as-Software' approach in Financial Shared Services, we offer agile models to fit your requirements.

Our strategy is based on a "Buy vs. Build" philosophy that aims to provide your company with a competitive advantage through minimizing time-to-market and avoiding the need for a new large-scale development.

Each engagement we pursue is a strategic partnership with our customers. Although we will call out specific engagements, you can be assured that we observe absolute confidentiality and never waiver on customer-focused data.

Data Integration Module for an Agentic AI Platform

An AI Platform company approached us for a Data Processing tool within the platform and a replacement to existing 3rd party tool. This AI platform has a huge reach in Petrochem to Life Sciences. Scale and Complexity handling were two key asks. We offered a custom Data Integration module that was part of Platform with same UX.

Platform Integration with Data Engines

A Data Platform company which is PE funded wanted to improve its valuation with an ‘Asset’ of its own in the platform and replace Hyper-Scalar Services and other legacy Products in Data Engineering and Data Governance. We White-Labeled the Tech Stack in a unique business model, alternative to complex IP acquisition. This now enabled Platform company with an On-premises In-House asset in the Valuation game.

Pre-Processing Data Module for a Functional & Domain Application

Big 4 Consulting firm had developed a great Financial Dashboard Framework for CXOs. But they were getting constrained with an expensive Cloud Services Cost and investments on human resourcing for onboarding new datasets manually. We offered an Integrated Automated Data Processing layer prior to their Dashboards.

Digital-Finance thru’ S-a-S

A Financial Transformation Agent, a CFO Advisory entity worked with us for a Digital Agent led Services-as-Software setup. This helped their customers to manage Data Consolidation, Calculation and Exception Reporting without depending on technical resources.

Customer 360° Data Lens

A Tech Consulting company wanted to pitch ‘Differently’ for their Enterprise Customers from Upper Right quadrant in Data Maturity league. They wanted a meaningful short Data Summary for CEO Office led by MarTech and Strategy wings. We built a Customer 360° Data solution, feeding data from all Tier 1 Software stacks which helped Marketing and Strategy team with a deeper Product / Customer view and helped making strategy and action plans.

‘No-Code’ Migration

A Premier Partner from HyperScalar community wanted to create a Migration Accelerator that would help them onboard Customers from different Legacy Tech stacks and make them consume Hyper-Scalar Services. Our Engines enabled Data Discovery and subsequent Migration.

Metadata led Data Governance

Very large Enterprise was using a Hyper-Scalar Services in Data Governance where there was a disconnect between Business Users and Pipeline creators. With our Searchable Metadata Manager, Business Users could adopt No-Code interface for defining Business logic on the data. This information was used by Data Engines to create Pipeline automatically.
