OUR STORY

We combine our passion with execution

Our Journey

We started our journey with a grand vision – democratizing data and digitally revolutionizing finance and business through an NLP-driven strategy. Although we got it right initially, commercially scaling our Analytics module was difficult with unavailability of Training Data.

This journey prompted us to prioritize our design to the core problem; Data Readiness before Automation, Analytics, and AI. With a vision to build a Data-First Environment, we came out with our own ETL framework.

As we delved deeper, we realized that Metadata is the actual driver of value. It's not only about onboarding and converting data but also about maximizing contextual discovery, enhancing data quality, and making intelligent governance possible. This realization compelled us to create a Metadata-driven Data Governance framework.

Meanwhile, we witnessed a significant change in the enterprise landscape - a strong wave of migration from Legacy Systems to the Cloud Platform, from Hyperscaler Services to Cloud Data Warehouse. Seeing the opportunity, we took our existing plugins and repurposed them to develop a Migration Engine.

Now with the philosophy of ReUsability of Technology, we have a whole platform from Ingestion to Insights. And when you read this Story, refer our Asset and go back to Foundry, you will be able to create the Business Case that we have built for you.

We are no longer merely data-rich; we are data-wise. Now that we've told our story, we'd love to hear yours – your investments, your challenges, and your plans on your data.
